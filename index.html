Our Mission

With growing concerns over the city's future, we at Vancouver Plan offer a platform for people to voice their opinion regarding major problems including income, housing and displacement. These three categories are what we, here at VancouverPlan, like to call the drivers of change. With these three drivers of change we can lay the foundation of the way to a brighter, more inclusive and happier city. We plan to shape the future of Vancouver using the thoughts, concerns and opinions of its citizens. Together we will tackle these problems hand in hand for a path towards a unified future. This cannot be done without the peoples help so please explore the site and let us know your opinions.
